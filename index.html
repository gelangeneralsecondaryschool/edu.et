About Gelan General Secondary School

Welcome to our Gelan General Secondary School
እንኳን ወደ ገላን አጠቃላይ ሁለተኛ ደረጃ ት/ቤት በደህና መጡ

  • Gelan General Secondary School, located in the dynamic area of Akaki Kality within the Gelan Condominium of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, is a distinguished educational institution. Since its establishment in 2010 (Ethiopian Calendar), the school has become a vital part of the community, renowned for its dedication to academic excellence and holistic student development.
  • Gelan General Secondary School provides a comprehensive curriculum that includes core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, English, and Social Studies, as well as electives like Art, Music, and Physical Education. The school is equipped with modern facilities, including state-of-the-art science laboratories, a computer lab, a well-stocked library, and sports grounds. The dedicated and experienced faculty at Gelan General Secondary School ensure that students receive a high-quality education, fostering both academic achievement and personal growth.

Discussion on Final Examination Preparations Held at Gelan General Secondary School

On January 8, 2017 EC, a meeting was held at Gelan General Secondary School among school principals to discuss the necessary preparations and preconditions for the upcoming final examinations.

During the discussion, key points were raised regarding the smooth and fair administration of the exams, ensuring students have a conducive environment for studying, and the measures needed to maintain exam integrity. The participants also emphasized the importance of proper scheduling, security, and supervision to uphold the credibility of the examination process.

Moreover, the meeting addressed the availability of essential resources, such as examination materials, seating arrangements, and invigilation procedures. The school administration reaffirmed its commitment to providing full support to ensure a successful examination period for all students.

At the conclusion of the discussion, school leaders agreed to implement the necessary steps promptly and work collaboratively to achieve an efficient and fair examination process.


City-Wide Grade 12 Model Examination Officially Begins

On January 6, 2017 EC, the city-wide Grade 12 Model Examination officially commenced in a peaceful and well-organized manner. This examination serves as a crucial preparatory step for students as they gear up for the upcoming national exams.

The examination is being conducted across various schools under strict supervision, ensuring fairness and discipline. School administrations, teachers, and examination coordinators have worked diligently to create a conducive testing environment that allows students to focus and perform to the best of their abilities. Furthermore, students are encouraged to utilize this opportunity to assess their readiness, refine their exam-taking strategies, and boost their confidence. The dedication and effort put into preparing for this examination reflect the commitment to academic excellence and future success.
We extend our best wishes to all Grade 12 students participating in this model examination. May their hard work and perseverance lead to outstanding results!


city-wide Grade 12 Model Examination

The city-wide Grade 12 Model Examination was successfully completed today, January 8, 2017 EC, at Gelan General Secondary School.

The examination process was conducted smoothly, ensuring a fair and disciplined environment for all students. Thanks to the collective efforts of teachers, administrators, and support staff, the event was well-organized and peaceful.

Congratulations to all the students for their dedication and hard work throughout the exam period. This marks an important step in their academic journey, helping them identify areas for improvement before the national exams.

Wishing all students success in their preparations for the final examinations!
