History and Administrative Details of Gelan General Secondary School

  • History and Administrative Details of Gelan General Secondary School
    Location: Gelan, Addis Ababa, Akaki Kality, Gelan Condominium, Ethiopia Established:2010 Ethiopian Calendar
  • Historical Overview Gelan General Secondary School, established in 2010 (Ethiopian Calendar) in Akaki Kality within the Gelan Condominium of Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, is dedicated to providing quality education to the local community. It has quickly become a cornerstone of academic excellence.

  • Mission and Vision
    - Mission: To provide a nurturing and intellectually stimulating environment that fosters academic excellence, creativity, and personal growth among students.
    - Vision: To become a leading educational institution in Ethiopia, producing well-rounded, socially responsible, and innovative individuals equipped to meet future challenges.

  • Academic Programs The school offers a comprehensive curriculum that includes: - Core Subjects: Mathematics, Science, English, Amharic, Social Studies. - Electives: Art, Music, Physical Education, Information Technology. - Extracurricular Activities:Sports, clubs, community service projects, and cultural events.

  • Facilities The school boasts modern facilities, including:
    - Classrooms: Spacious and well-equipped.
    - Science Laboratories: State-of-the-art labs.
    - Computer Lab: Latest technology.
    - Library: A rich collection of resources.
    - Sports Grounds: Facilities for football, basketball, and athletics.

    • Faculty The dedicated and experienced faculty at Gelan General Secondary School are passionate about teaching and committed to student success. Regular professional development ensures that teachers stay updated with the latest educational practices.
  • Community Engagement

    The school maintains strong community ties through:
    - Parent-Teacher Associations: Regular meetings.
    - Community Service:Projects that develop social responsibility.
    - Collaborations:Partnerships with local organizations and businesses.
  • Academic Achievements
    Gelan General Secondary School consistently achieves high academic standards, as evidenced by students' performance in national examinations and extracurricular activities.

  • Future Goals
    - Expand Facilities: New classrooms, labs, and recreational areas.
    - Innovate Curriculum:Advanced technology and new teaching methods.
    - Enhance Community Programs: Strengthen community involvement.
    - Foster Global Awareness: Prepare students to be global citizens through diverse and inclusive education.

    • Our Vision

    At Gelan General Secondary School, our vision is to become a leading educational institution in Ethiopia, recognized for academic excellence, holistic development, and innovation. We aim to nurture well-rounded, socially responsible individuals who are prepared to meet future challenges and contribute positively to society.
